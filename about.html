Our Journey

From Cell to Global Ministry

2018

The Beginning: Mime Chime

Louder fellowship started way back in 2018 as a cell, later transformed into a fellowship under the name 'mime chime' to mean living water fellowship. Led by Prophet Ezekiel Kayondo, the vision was clear from the start: to win souls and equip saints.

2020

A New Revelation: GOD SPEAKS LOUDER

With time the name Louder was introduced due to a revelation that God speaks loudly, not just in small still voices. In 2020, due to a need to provide believers with a place of continual mentorship and growth, one they would call home, Louder started a church under the name 'Light The Fire Again', marking the beginning of Sunday services.

"When the COVID pandemic hit in 2020 and we could not meet physically, the man of God was led by the Spirit to start online meetings. This expanded our reach to outside countries, the army, police, schools, and people from all over the country."

2022

The Birth of REHOBOTH

In 2022, during an anniversary event, a prophetic word was released that the church would receive land soon. This led to a campaign to buy land and the need for formal registration. After a long scuffle with the registration bureau where previous names were rejected, the name REHOBOTH was born.

Rehoboth Discipleship Global Ministries represents the room God has made for us to flourish in the land.

Now

Impact & Expansion

Today, Louder is a thriving community based in Kampala, Kasubi-Kawaala. Having completed the purchase of our first piece of land, we are on a journey to expand, develop, and create more impact. Below are some achievements God has graced us with.

  • Rehoboth: Our first church, with land in Kasubi
  • Over 300 children in Sunday school
  • Global Cell Program
  • Nourish: School of Ministry
  • Global Zoom Fellowships
  • Baruch - School of Christian Writers
  • Medical outreaches
  • Nationwide Missions
