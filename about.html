Our Journey

From Cell to Global Ministry

2015

The Beginning: Mayim Chayim

We began in 2015 with a Bible study of three people meeting in a corridor at Bulamba Hostel in Makerere Kivulu. That small gathering grew into a fellowship called Mayim Chayim — a Hebrew phrase meaning Living Water — where we would gather on a veranda to hear the Word of God and pray.

The Ministry Vision

God then gave our vision bearer, Ezekiel Kayondo, a vision during a time of prayer and fasting. In it, God showed him multitudes of people moving along the road from Kasubi to Makerere University Western gate — a sign that He was sending us to that area, and that many would turn to Him through our service there.

2018

New revelation - God Speaks LOUDER

In 2018, following a series of outdoor all-night prayer meetings at the MTR grounds in Makerere University, alongside cell meetings and room fellowships, God directed us to start Louder Fellowship. God declared, "I speak louder" — that His voice is not merely a whisper, but audible, loud, and clear — and we were called to proclaim and demonstrate exactly that through prophetic teaching.

The fellowship began by faith. It moved from a rented restaurant, to a rented hall, to a rented garage, to a rented house, to an offered office, then through two rented schools consecutively — and now to our own land in Kampala, Kasubi–Kawaala, and beyond to the nations.

2020

Expansion to the Nations

When the COVID-19 pandemic struck in 2020 and physical gatherings were no longer possible, our man of God was led by the Holy Spirit to launch online meetings. This expanded our reach beyond Uganda to other nations, and into the army, police, schools, and communities across the country.

2022

Planting Rehoboth

In 2022, God led us to plant a church in Kasubi, Kampala, in fulfilment of the heavenly vision. That church was initially called Light the Fire Again, as its mandate was to rekindle passion and love for Christ in the surrounding community. That church is now officially registered as Rehoboth Discipleship Global Ministries, and God has blessed it with land and a large home.

Rehoboth means "God has made room for us to flourish and be fruitful in the land" — and that is precisely what is happening.

Now

A Global Mission

Over the past 8 to 10 years, by the grace and presence of God, accompanied by miracles, signs, and wonders, we have grown from a small Bible study into a ministry reaching communities, television stations, radio stations, Zoom meetings, social media platforms, institutions of learning, and nations — touching every tongue and reaching kings and commoners alike.

Our sound has gone out to

Makerere, Kikoni, Nankulabye, Kasubi, Kawaala, Namungona, Kampala, Mukono, Mbarara, Lutete, Mengo, Kisasi, Mpererwe, Entebbe, Nansana, Bombo, Budo, Mbale, Nigeria, Somalia, Dubai, Canada, Turkey, Germany, France, the United Kingdom, the USA — and we are still preaching Christ, louder!

Below are some achievements God has graced us with.

  • Rehoboth: Our first church, with land in Kasubi
  • Children's ministry: Over 300 children in Sunday school
  • Global Cell Program
  • Nourish: School of Ministry
  • Global Zoom Fellowships
  • Baruch - School of Christian Writers
  • Medical outreaches
  • Nationwide Gospel Missions
  • Opharnage
  • Children's Mission Centre
  • Kings and Priests Christian School
  • School Outreaches

Through annual missions, charity outreaches, school missions, church and altar planting, community cell gatherings, institutional outreaches, the Nourish Bible School, the Baruch School of Christian Writers, weekly children's cells, intercessor gatherings, worship events, equipment classes, books, and prayer walks — our vision remains: to win souls to Jesus Christ and to equip the saints in Him who is the Head — Christ.
